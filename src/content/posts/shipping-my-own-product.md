# Shipping my first app after years of building for others

There is a different kind of pressure when the product is yours. The technical work matters, but the harder part is deciding what is enough to ship.

After years working inside larger teams, I am using this space to document the shift toward smaller experiments, faster feedback, and clearer product bets.

## What I am optimizing for

- A small scope that can be finished.
- A technical foundation that does not fight maintenance.
- A feedback loop that produces decisions, not just more ideas.

The goal is not to make every project perfect. The goal is to keep shipping and learn in public.
