# Getting inspired to ship after a RevenueCat CTO talk

Good technical talks are useful when they change what you do the next week.

The biggest reminder I took away was simple: strong engineering matters most when it helps a product reach people faster and with fewer avoidable failures.

## Notes I want to keep

- Make the first version smaller.
- Protect the core user path.
- Keep infrastructure boring until the product proves it needs more.
- Write about the work so the learning compounds.

That is the direction for this site: a public record of building, learning, and shipping.
